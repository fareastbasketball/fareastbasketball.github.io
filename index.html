About Us

Volunteerism and the Society

Far East Basketball is a non-profit organization, it was founded in March 2020 by a group of volunteer parents who are committed to serving the youth in the community through sports of basketball. The Group was formed because we experienced what other parents do, which their children are left in sports of basketball because of its accessibility and cost.

    Our Goal

  • Lower the cost associated.
  • Promote the value of sportsmanship,teamwork and respect to each other.
  • Boost the athlete confidence needed, either inside or outside of basketball court.
  • Elevate their game and work ethics through different challenging skills training.

Far East Basketball is committed to giving back all to the community by taking care of the youth by promoting the importance of society and physical activity through fun, friendly games, and practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I register my child?

    Each season has its Registration form attached when you click the register in the Pricing tab. After submitting your interest, we will send an email regarding the instruction on how to pay the Fee. If you need payment assistance send us an email at fareastbtl@nucleus.com

  • What time and days do the practice and games held?

    We book the gym time from 6:30 pm to 8:30 pm. Each team has a maximum of 1 hour of practice x 2 times per week, this includes the skill development and team coaching for the in-house league, and for the days, as we will advise the schedule before the season starts.

  • Where are the games and practice will be held?

    Gym locations are to be announced once we finalized the availability.

  • During basketball season, do you use an app for announcing the game and practice time ?

    Yes, we are using Heja app, you can download it via google play or apple store, once your children are in their designated team, coaches will add you to their list of players, so everyone will receive the updated schedule and mark your availability.

  • Does the Far East Basketball is a member of Alberta sport organization?

    Yes, Far East Basketball Training League is a member of Alberta Basketball Association. We are governed by ABA Policy

  • I have further question, how do I contact the group

    Please use the form under the contact and we will answer your question on or the following day.
